Company History
For the past 6 years, we here at Willie and Willy's have been a one stop shop for everything a writer could possibly need. Our dedicated team is passionate about helping all writers, from the hobbyist to the professional reporter, with all of their writing needs. We provide a large selection of products for our customers such as the following:
  • pens
  • pencils
  • paper
  • white-out
  • Associated Press Guides
  • Complimentary Resume Templates
Hold on! We don't stop there. We also offer writers with comprehensive support help take their writing to the next level. Just couple of our services include in house editing and proofreading. Come check us out. We'd be happy to help you with any of your writing needs.


Our History
Our dedicated founder Ellie Zaczek formed Willie and Willy's about 6 years ago on a cold winter day in West Lafayette when she was working with 2 young writers (Willie and Willy). She has slowly built team of Purdue University students who use their talents together to make the world of writing a better place. Over the past 6 years, we've taken the writing world by storm. We started our revolutionary journey by just selling writing supplies, and Ellie would roam the store getting to know the writers using our products. Writers grew to love the store's atmoshpere and talking to Ellie. Before she knew it, the store was selling out of its entire inventory., so going into our second year, we expanded our operations. We secured a wholesale supplier and warehouse space, and we more than doubled the amount of products we were able to move. With so many writers coming to us for equipment, Ellie decided to ask them how we could better help them, and they wanted help with their writing. In our third year, we released our trademark writing support services. The writers loved it! Since then, we've been meeting with writers around the clock helping improve their writer and prepare for those future publisher meetings. Today, We have the following 5-person management team:
